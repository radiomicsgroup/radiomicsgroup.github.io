Data sets

This page lists data sets that are made available as part of the research and publications of the Radiomics group.

Freely available data

  • Histology-informed microstructural diffusion simulations for MRI cancer characterisation (Histo-µSim): ex vivo mouse data. Grussu F, Grigoriou A, Macarro C, Perez-Lopez R. Zenodo 2024a, doi: 10.5281/zenodo.14559356. The data set consists of ex vivo 9.4T MRI of fixed mouse specimens with co-localised histological sections, stained for hematoxylin-eosin (HE).
  • Histology-informed microstructural diffusion simulations for MRI cancer characterisation (Histo-µSim): histology substrates Grigoriou, A., Macarro, C., Voronova, A., Perez-Lopez, R., & Grussu, F. Zenodo 2024b, doi: 10.5281/zenodo.14559104. The data set consists of 18 3D geometric models of cancer tissues in SVG and PLY format reconstructed from HE biopsies.

Data available upon request

  • Ex vivo 9.4T MRI of fixed mouse livers with co-localised histological sections stained with HE. Data used in F Grussu et al, Magnetic Resonance in Medicine 2022, 88(1): 365-379; doi: 10.1002/mrm.29174. To access the data contact Francesco Grussu or Raquel Pérez López.
