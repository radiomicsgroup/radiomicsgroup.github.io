This page lists data sets that are made available as part of the research and publications of the Radiomics group. 

### Freely available data
- _Histology-informed microstructural diffusion simulations for MRI cancer characterisation (Histo-µSim): ex vivo mouse data_. Grussu F, Grigoriou A, Macarro C, Perez-Lopez R. Zenodo 2024a, doi: [10.5281/zenodo.14559356](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14559356). The data set consists of _ex vivo_ 9.4T MRI of fixed mouse specimens with co-localised histological sections, stained for hematoxylin-eosin (HE).
- _Histology-informed microstructural diffusion simulations for MRI cancer characterisation (Histo-µSim): histology substrates_ Grigoriou, A., Macarro, C., Voronova, A., Perez-Lopez, R., & Grussu, F. Zenodo 2024b, doi: [10.5281/zenodo.14559104](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14559104). The data set consists of 18 3D geometric models of cancer tissues in SVG and PLY format reconstructed from HE biopsies. 


### Data available upon request
- _Ex vivo_ 9.4T MRI of fixed mouse livers with co-localised histological sections stained with HE. Data used in F Grussu et al, *Magnetic Resonance in Medicine* 2022, 88(1): 365-379; doi: [10.1002/mrm.29174](https://doi.org/10.1002/mrm.29174). To access the data contact [Francesco Grussu](mailto:fgrussu@vhio.net) or [Raquel Pérez López](mailto:rperez@vhio.net).
